PBP 2019

23 Aug 2019 - Less than 1 minute read

I went into PBP thinking it’d be something I’d do once and probably never again, but I now see why so many folks come back every 4 years. There is nothing I’ve seen that even comes close. I’ve been trying to put it into words but I can’t. And these photos do nothing to capture the magic of PBP. All I can say is that I’ll be back, and if you have even the slightest bit of interest you should do it. You’ve got 4 years to get ready :) Oh, and Charly Hands is the way to go. Crush watts/wine like @genericbeckham and you’ll have a great time.
