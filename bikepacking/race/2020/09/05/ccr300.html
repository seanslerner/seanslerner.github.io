Cascadian Crossing Route 300

05 Sep 2020 - Less than 1 minute read

I’m still gathering my thoughts on the Cascadian Crossing Route 300, but my top 5 favorite / least favorite parts were:

Top 5

  • descending Santiam (once it got good) during golden hour
  • the climb out of Cougar Reservoir
  • Alpine to Westfir
  • descending Fuji
  • Metolius-Windigo after Waldo

Bottom 5

  • Bunchgrass
  • walking downhill in sand on Santiam (before it got rideable)
  • Waldo
  • the ~5 miles before Sisters
  • COD just before Bend
